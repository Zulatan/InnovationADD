INTENDED USE

1. To turn on the fan - insert the cable with the built in usb2.0/3.0 into your desired device.

2. Go to the app "FAN-tastic" and connect to the fan with bluetooth.

3. From there, you are told to set your prefered temperature settings.

4. You only need to do this once, and then the fan will remember your settings
(You can change your setting whenever you want to)

5. From now on, you can use the fan by turning it on either on the fan or with the app.
